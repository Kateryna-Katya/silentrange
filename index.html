Silent Killer: Hoistway Havoc

Ride the Car Top. Throw Tools. Survive the Counterweight.

A blue-collar arcade shooter set inside a living hoistway.
You’re an elevator mechanic holding the line against chaos. Drift the car.
Auto-fire tools. Dodge citations. Stack wages. Face bosses. And above all: never touch the counterweight. Look, we mixed elevator jokes with arcade shooting because honestly? Who else was gonna do it? Made by mechanics, for mechanics.

Built for the trade. Respectfully chaotic.
